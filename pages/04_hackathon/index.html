#ShockTheWeb⚡️ is a virtual hackathon to promote, explore, build and design web applications that can interact with WebLN enabled wallets and browsers. Our goal is to make building on bitcoin and lightning more accessible to web developers, decreasing essential onboarding time and creating a fun, collaborative environment for both bitcoin devs and designers.

Bitcoin development can seem scary for new developers coming in, but it doesn't have to be. With the lightning network's toolkit and libraries a bunch of new opportunities are waiting to be explored. You'll get a primer on some important low level bitcoin concepts, but the goal is to make things and have fun!

During the hackathon we'll have be a bunch of guided sprint modules and sessions, workshops and talks, as well as ongoing mentorship from some friendly bitcoiner developers and designers to help you make your app or prototype.
